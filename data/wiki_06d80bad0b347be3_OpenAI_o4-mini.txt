OpenAI o4-mini is a generative pre-trained transformer model created by OpenAI . On April 16, 2025, the o4-mini model was released to all ChatGPT users (including free-tier users) as well as via the Chat Completions API and Responses API. [ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ]
Additionally, OpenAI introduced the o4-mini-high model, which was made available exclusively to paid-tier ChatGPT users. The high model offers more advanced features, including higher response accuracy and faster processing times. [ 5 ]
Unlike earlier models, o4-mini is capable of processing both text and images. It also allows to perform tasks like analyzing whiteboard sketches during its "chain-of-thought" phase. [ 6 ]
o4-mini API providers says that it's designed to enhance decision-making across sectors by enabling utilities to forecast demand and analyze infrastructure data, supporting healthcare through extraction and interpretation of medical records and diagnostics, and assisting financial institutions with real-time regulatory compliance and risk assessment through automated document analysis and data processing. [ 7 ] A study shows that the o4-mini model has significantly improved its ability to complete tasks in the field of chemistry compared to the GPT-3 model, but there is still a gap compared to other models (Such as Deepseek-R1 model). [ 8 ]
